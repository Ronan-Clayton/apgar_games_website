Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7th November 2024 1. Introduction Welcome to Sudoku Warrior, a game developed by Apgar Games Ltd. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS game app, Sudoku Warrior. By accessing or using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. 2. Information We Collect a. Personal Information Game Center Authentication: We use Apple’s Game Center to authenticate users. We collect your Game Center Player ID and alias to identify you within the game. In-App Purchases: All transactions are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any personal payment information. b. Gameplay Data Level Data: Information about the levels you attempt, time taken to complete levels, and power-ups used. User Progress Data: Your ELO rating, number of levels attempted, and the number of lives remaining. c. Location Data Approximate Location: We may collect coarse location data to understand where our app is being used and to assess its international reach. This data is not precise and cannot be used to identify your exact location. d. Future Data Collection Interaction Data: While not currently collected, we may in the future collect data on how you interact with app features to enhance user experience. Error Reports: We currently collect server-side crash reports to improve app stability and performance and may collect app error and crash reports in the future to improve performance further. 3. How We Use Your Information Enhancing Gameplay Experience: Personalizing content and features based on your gameplay patterns. Progress Tracking: Keeping track of your progress to provide seamless gameplay. Improvement and Development: Using data to fix bugs, improve app performance, and develop new features. Communication: Sending you push notifications about updates, new features, or promotional offers. You can opt out of these notifications in your device settings. Analytics: Understanding the geographical distribution of our users to evaluate international reach and popularity. 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure a. Third-Party Service Providers AWS Services: We utilize Amazon Web Services (AWS) for data storage and processing: AWS Cognito: Manages user authentication and access to AWS services. AWS Lambda & DynamoDB: Handle server-side logic and store user data securely. Game Center: Authentication and basic profile information are managed through Apple’s Game Center. b. Legal Requirements We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities. 5. Data Security We implement security measures to protect your data: Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. Access Controls: Only authenticated users can access certain features of the app. AWS Security Measures: We rely on AWS's robust security protocols for server-side data protection. 6. Your Rights Access and Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting us at Support@SudokuWarrior.app and providing your Game Center information for verification. Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data by emailing us at Support@SudokuWarrior.app with your Game Center details. Opt-Out Options: Push Notifications: You can opt out via your device settings. Location Data: You can disable location services in your device settings. Continued Data Collection: Certain data is essential for gameplay. Opting out of this data collection may limit app functionality. 7. Children's Privacy Our app is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed. 8. International Users We offer our app in multiple countries and comply with relevant data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union. 9. Automated Decision-Making We use algorithms for matchmaking based on ELO ratings to enhance your gaming experience. This automated decision-making is essential for fair and competitive gameplay. 10. Data Retention We retain your data as long as you use the app. Upon your request for deletion, we will promptly remove your data from our servers. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. 12. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us: Email: Support@SudokuWarrior.app Twitter: @_sudokuwarrior Apgar Games Ltd is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. We comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including the European Digital Services Act (DSA) and the Apple Paid Apps Agreement.
